OUR STORY

Welcome to Italiano Pizza & Burgers

Located in the heart of Satellite Town, Rawalpindi, Italiano Pizza and Burgers has been serving authentic Italian flavors with a desi twist since 2018. Our chefs craft every pizza with hand-tossed dough, fresh ingredients, and a passion for perfection.

From our signature Bar BQ Pizza to the legendary Zinger Burger, every dish tells a story of quality and taste. We're not just a restaurant — we're a destination for food lovers.
